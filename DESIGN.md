# Bliz E-commerce Design System

## Overview
Professional Ukrainian e-commerce design inspired by modern online stores like Comfy, Rozetka, and Citrus.

## Color Palette

### Brand Colors
- **Primary Green**: `#2abd13` - Main brand color for buttons, links, accents
- **Green Dark**: `#239c0f` - Hover states
- **Green Light**: `#eaf7e4` - Backgrounds, highlights

### Accent Colors
- **Orange**: `#fc5e0f` - Promotions, special offers
- **Red**: `#f44336` - Discounts, errors
- **Blue**: `#0b6ac1` - Information, new items

### Grayscale
- Black to White spectrum for text and backgrounds
- Gray variations for borders and subtle elements

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Base Size**: 14px (1.4rem)
- **Weights**: 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold), 800 (extrabold)

## Component Structure

### Product Card
- Clean white background with subtle shadow
- Hover effect: lift + enhanced shadow
- Image: 1:1 aspect ratio with contain fit
- Action buttons: wishlist, compare (top-right)
- Labels: new, hit, sale (top-left)
- Rating with stars
- Price with old price strikethrough
- Discount badge
- Credit info
- Bonus points
- Green "Add to Cart" button

### Header
- Two-level structure:
  - Top bar: links, contacts (hidden on mobile)
  - Main header: logo, catalog button, search, actions
- Sticky positioning
- Shadow for depth

### Buttons
- Primary: Green background, white text
- Rounded corners (0.8rem)
- Hover: darken + lift
- Shadow for depth

### Forms
- 2px border
- Green focus state with shadow
- Rounded inputs
- Clear labels

## Responsive Breakpoints
- Desktop: 1200px+
- Tablet: 768px - 1199px
- Mobile: < 768px

## Animations
- Smooth transitions (0.3s ease)
- Hover effects on cards
- Button interactions
- Modal appearances

## Design Principles
1. **Clean & Modern**: White space, clear hierarchy
2. **Green Brand**: Consistent use of primary color
3. **User-Friendly**: Large touch targets, clear actions
4. **Professional**: Quality shadows, smooth transitions
5. **Ukrainian Style**: Following local e-commerce conventions
